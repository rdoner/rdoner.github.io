About Me

Ryan has five years of software development experience utilizing JavaScript, React, React Native, Angular, Node, SQL, and AWS cloud architecture. After being exposed to programming in his computational neuroscience classes, he fell in love with the process of learning computer science and decided to pursue a career in software engineering.

Ryan got his start in the biotech industry building lab automation software, then learned web development through an apprenticeship at Techtonic. Using his new skills in Javascript, he worked on a variety of fullstack projects at CenturyLink, Lockheed Martin, and Finastra. He enjoys utilizing his background in neuroscience to learn and teach computer science difficult concepts to fellow engineers.
